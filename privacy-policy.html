PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: April 2026

At Zen Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, we respect and are committed to protecting your privacy. When you register for classes, sign up for newsletters, or contact us, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you voluntarily provide.

Payment Information:

If you make a purchase, we collect billing and payment information such as credit card details through secure third-party payment processors. We do not store credit card information on our servers.

Website Usage Data:

We may collect non-personal information about your interaction with our website, including your IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and the date and time of your visit. This data helps us improve our website and provide a better user experience.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. Cookies help us analyze trends, track user movements, and gather demographic information.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

To Provide Services:

We use your personal information to process class registrations, schedule lessons, and communicate with you about updates, promotions, or important information related to Zen Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.

Marketing and Communication:

We may use your email address and phone number to send you newsletters, promotional offers, or other marketing materials. You can opt out of these communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in the email.

Website Improvement and Analytics:

We use cookies and website usage data to improve our website's functionality and to analyze visitor trends for marketing purposes. This information helps us tailor content and advertisements to users.

DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

Service Providers:

We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist in operating our website, processing payments, or sending communications. These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and only use it for the services they provide.

Legal Requirements:

We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect our rights, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your personal information may be transferred as part of the business transaction. You will be notified via email and/or a prominent notice on our website of any change in ownership or uses of your personal information.

  • Access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request that we correct any inaccuracies in your personal information.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of marketing communications at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in the emails we send you or contacting us directly.

SECURITY OF YOUR INFORMATION

We take the security of your personal information seriously.
