KIDS BRAZILIAN JIU-JITSU

Building Confidence, Discipline & Self-Defense Skills in a Fun, Safe Environment

WHY BJJ FOR KIDS?

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu isn't just a martial art — it's one of the most powerful tools available for child development. Our kids programs are specifically designed to harness the physical and mental benefits of BJJ in a fun, safe, and structured environment.

  • ✅ Anti-Bullying Skills — Real confidence and de-escalation tools
  • ✅ Focus & Discipline — Translates directly to academic performance
  • ✅ Respect & Responsibility — Core values woven into every class
  • ✅ Physical Fitness — Coordination, strength, and cardiovascular health
  • ✅ Teamwork & Social Skills — Build genuine friendships on the mat
  • ✅ Belt Progression System — Motivating milestone achievement

PROGRAMS FOR EVERY AGE

Our curriculum is thoughtfully divided by age to ensure every child gets instruction that matches their developmental stage.

🥷

LITTLE NINJAS

Ages 4–6

Introductory program focused on motor skills, body awareness, listening, and fundamental movement patterns through games and fun activities.

⭐

YOUTH BJJ

Ages 7–12

Core curriculum covering fundamental techniques: stranger danger awareness, basic takedowns, positional grappling, guard, mount, and the BJJ belt progression system.

🏆

TEEN BJJ

Ages 13–17

Advanced technical instruction preparing teens for adult competition and advanced belt ranks. Emphasizes strategy, conditioning, and real-world self-defense scenarios.

WHAT YOUR CHILD WILL LEARN

Our structured curriculum ensures every student builds a strong foundation and progresses confidently through the ranks.

01

SAFETY & STRANGER DANGER

Learn to recognize unsafe situations and practice verbal and physical boundary-setting skills that could one day save their life.

02

FUNDAMENTAL MOVEMENT

Shrimping, bridging, rolling, and positional movement — the building blocks of all BJJ techniques, taught in a fun and engaging way.

03

BASIC TAKEDOWNS

Age-appropriate takedowns drawn from wrestling and Judo that safely and effectively bring an opponent to the ground.

04

POSITIONAL GRAPPLING

Guard, mount, side control, and back control — learning to maintain advantageous positions in a controlled, safe environment.

05

SUBMISSIONS & DEFENSE

Safe, age-appropriate submission techniques and how to defend against them — building awareness and technical skill simultaneously.

06

BELT PROGRESSION

A clear, motivating path from white belt through colored belts with regular belt testing ceremonies that celebrate achievement.
